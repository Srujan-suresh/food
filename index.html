Frequently Asked Questions

~ Are the chapattis freshly made every day?

Yes, all our chapattis are freshly prepared at home every day using quality ingredients to ensure softness, taste, and hygiene.

No, we do not use preservatives or artificial additives. Our chapattis are 100% home-made and prepared in a traditional way.
